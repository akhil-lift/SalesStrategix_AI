================================================================================

## THE "ANCHOR AND ADJUSTMENT" TECHNIQUE
Always anchor to the $420K savings we provide compared to a fragmented stack.
"Most companies pay $600/user for this stack. We are providing it for $199."

## BRACKETING
Never give a single discount percentage. Use brackets like 5-15% tied to
commitment terms (e.g., 2-year prepay for max discount).

## THE "SILENCE" CLOSE
After stating the price, stop talking. Let the AI 'RTC' card remind you to
stay silent.

- RTC: Real-Time Coaching
- AI-First: Native intelligence from the ground up.
- Pipeline Hygiene: The accuracy of your deal data.
- Revenue Intelligence: Data-driven sales insights.
- Deal Slippage: When a deal closes after its expected date.
- Ramp Velocity: The speed at which a new hire becomes profitable.
- Economic Buyer: The person with the power to sign the check.

- SaaS: Software as a Service.

- GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation.
- API: Application Programming Interface.
- SSO: Single Sign-On.
- CSM: Customer Success Manager.
- QBR: Quarterly Business Review.
- SLA: Service Level Agreement.
- BAA: Business Associate Agreement.
