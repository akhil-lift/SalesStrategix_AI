# Sales Playbook: Objection Handling & Pricing Guidelines

## Objection: "Your price is too high"
Strategy: Value-Based Selling.
- Acknowledge the concern: "I understand that budget is a key consideration."
- Reframe as ROI: "Let's look at the cost of inaction. Most clients see a 20% efficiency gain in the first quarter."
- Focus on unique differentiators: Mention our proprietary AI analytics which competitors lack.

## Objection: "We are happy with our current vendor"
Strategy: Disruption & Differentiation.
- "It's great that you have a solution in place. Many of our clients came from [Competitor] specifically because they needed better real-time reporting."
- Offer a comparison: "Would you be open to a 15-minute side-by-side audit of your current playbook performance?"

## Pricing Guidelines
- Standard Tier: $500/user/month.
- Enterprise Tier: Custom pricing based on volume.
- Discounting: Max 10% discount for annual upfront payments. Any higher requires VP approval.

## Negotiation Tactics
1. Never give without a get: If they ask for a discount, ask for a multi-year commitment.
2. Build rapport: Research the client's recent LinkedIn posts before the call.
3. Use silence: After presenting the price, wait for their reaction.
