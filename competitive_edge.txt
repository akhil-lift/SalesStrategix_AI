# SalesStrategix: Competitive Edge & Support Guide

## Competitive Comparison: Salesforce
Strategy: Focus on Agility and AI-First approach.
- "Salesforce is a great legacy platform, but it often requires expensive consultants to customize."
- "SalesStrategix is built on an AI-First architecture, meaning you get automated insights out of the box without the 6-month implementation delay."
- Key Differentiator: Our real-time sentiment analysis during calls, which Salesforce lacks in its standard tiers.

## Support Service Level Agreements (SLA)
- Standard Support: 24-hour response time (Business Days).
- Enterprise Support: 4-hour response time (24/7).
- Critical Bugs: We aim for a workaround within 8 hours for all Enterprise clients.
